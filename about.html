About GharKaMali

Professional Gardening Services for Urban Homes & Societies

GharKaMali is a professional gardening and plant maintenance service designed for modern urban living. We help homeowners, apartment residents, and societies maintain healthy, beautiful green spaces without the stress of daily plant care.

Our Mission

To make urban living greener and healthier by providing reliable, professional, and hassle-free gardening services for homes and societies, using sustainable practices and trained experts.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted urban gardening service, transforming balconies, terraces, and communities into thriving green spaces—one home, one society, and one city at a time.

Easy - Professional - Sustainable

Solving the Urban Green Crisis

With increasing urbanisation in cities like Noida, Greater Noida West, and Gurugram, many homes struggle to keep plants alive due to lack of time, proper knowledge, and reliable gardeners.

GharKaMali was created to solve this problem through structured, subscription-based gardening services. Our trained gardeners provide regular plant care, soil management, fertilisation, and overall maintenance.

"At GharKaMali, our goal is simple: to make home gardening easy, professional, and sustainable for every urban household."
